Defining the Standard in Risk Resilience

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is a premier industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to closing the talent gap in digital, cyber, and technology risk roles. We focus on preparing professionals for the most demanding, regulated, and high-consequence environments.

By forging powerful partnerships with leading UK universities and top-tier industry players, IDR bridges the critical gap between academic insight and real-world execution. Our cohorts don't just learn theory; they solve actual industry challenges before they even graduate.
